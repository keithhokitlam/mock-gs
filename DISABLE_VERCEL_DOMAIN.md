# Disable/Redirect Vercel Default Domain (mock-gs.vercel.app)

## Option 1: Redirect to Custom Domain (Recommended)

This redirects anyone who visits `mock-gs.vercel.app` to your custom domain `grocery-share.com`.

### Steps:

1. **Go to Vercel Dashboard:**
   - Visit: https://vercel.com/dashboard
   - Click on your **grocery-share.com** project

2. **Go to Domain Settings:**
   - Click **Settings** tab
   - Click **Domains** in the left sidebar

3. **Add the .vercel.app domain:**
   - Click **"Add Domain"** or **"Add"**
   - Enter: `mock-gs.vercel.app`
   - Click **Add**

4. **Configure Redirect:**
   - After adding, click on `mock-gs.vercel.app` in the list
   - Select **"Redirect to Another Domain"**
   - Enter your custom domain: `grocery-share.com` (or `www.grocery-share.com` if you prefer)
   - Select redirect type: **307 Temporary Redirect** (or 301 Permanent Redirect)
   - Click **Save**

5. **Result:**
   - Anyone visiting `mock-gs.vercel.app` will be automatically redirected to `grocery-share.com`
   - The old domain still works but redirects to your custom domain

---

## Option 2: Leave It As-Is

- The `.vercel.app` domain will continue to work
- It won't interfere with your custom domain
- You can just ignore it - most users won't know about it
- No action needed

---

## Option 3: Update Project Name (Changes the .vercel.app URL)

If you want to change the `.vercel.app` domain name:

1. **Go to Vercel Dashboard:**
   - Click on your project
   - Go to **Settings** â†’ **General**

2. **Change Project Name:**
   - Find **"Project Name"** field
   - Change it from `mock-gs` to something else (e.g., `grocery-share`)
   - Click **Save**

3. **Result:**
   - Your new `.vercel.app` domain will be `grocery-share.vercel.app`
   - The old `mock-gs.vercel.app` will stop working
   - You can then redirect the new one if you want

---

## Recommendation

**Use Option 1 (Redirect)** - This is the cleanest approach:
- Redirects old links/bookmarks to your custom domain
- Maintains SEO (301/307 redirects pass link equity)
- Users always end up on your branded domain
- The `.vercel.app` domain still works for testing if needed

---

## Note

You **cannot completely delete** the `.vercel.app` domain - it's automatically generated by Vercel for every project. The best you can do is redirect it to your custom domain.
